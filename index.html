Motion

An advanced minecraft bot for 1.19.3 - 1.8
Motion is a open source, botting tool used for Minecraft: Java Edition and uses Mineflayer for the bot. Motion has a wide variety of features and options to choose from, ranging from application appearance to bot behaviors. Motion plans to achieve most features that popular Minecraft Bots have such as OQ.Minebot or mcbot.xyz.
Motion is also a fork of TrafficerMC by RattlesHyper and will be updated bi-weekly by gasps.
Note: Motion will be an ongoing project for 6-8 months and then will be discontinued
Why choose Motion?
* Feature o' plenty!
* Motion is updated and bug checked every week!
* Suggestions for features/ideas will be added from Motion users!
* Sleek & Clean GUI Design and Easy to Use!
* Open source and fully customizable to your liking!
Motion's system requirements are minium of 4GB RAM, 5th Generation Processor, and Intel HD Graphics. (Not required) Motion is only available on Windows & Linux, macOS support is unavailable
